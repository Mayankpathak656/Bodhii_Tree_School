About The Bodhii Tree School

Creating magical learning experiences since 2008

Our Story

The Bodhii Tree School was founded in 2008 by Mrs. Sarah Williams, a passionate early childhood educator with over 20 years of experience. What started as a small home-based preschool with just 8 children has grown into a thriving learning community serving over 200 families.

Our journey began with a simple belief: every child deserves a magical place where learning is fun, creativity is encouraged, and each little personality is nurtured with love and care. Today, we continue to uphold these values while embracing modern educational approaches.

Over the years, we've helped thousands of children take their first steps into the world of learning, building confidence, friendships, and a lifelong love for education.

Our Beautiful Campus

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing, creative, and stimulating environment where every child can discover their unique potential, develop essential life skills, and build a strong foundation for future learning through play-based education.

Our Vision

To be the leading early childhood education center that inspires young minds to become confident, creative, and compassionate individuals who are ready to embrace the world with curiosity and joy.
