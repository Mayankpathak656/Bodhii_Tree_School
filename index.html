Our Mission

At The Bodhii Tree School, we believe that every child is unique and deserves a nurturing environment where they can explore, learn, and grow at their own pace. Our play-based curriculum combines fun activities with educational goals to prepare children for their future learning journey.

Our Programs

Age-appropriate learning experiences designed for every stage

Toddler Program

Ages 18 months - 2.5 years

Gentle introduction to structured play, sensory activities, and basic social skills in a warm, caring environment.

  • Sensory play activities
  • Music and movement
  • Basic motor skills
  • Social interaction

Preschool Program

Ages 2.5 - 4 years

Comprehensive early learning program focusing on cognitive development, creativity, and school readiness.

  • Pre-reading and pre-math skills
  • Creative arts and crafts
  • Science exploration
  • Independence building

Pre-K Program

Ages 4 - 5 years

Advanced preparation for kindergarten with focus on academic readiness, critical thinking, and leadership skills.

  • Reading and writing readiness
  • Mathematical concepts
  • Problem-solving skills
  • Leadership development
