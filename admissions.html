Admissions

Join The Bodhii Tree School family and give your child the best start in life

Admission Process

Simple steps to enroll your child at The Bodhii Tree School

1

Inquiry & Visit

Contact us to schedule a school visit and learn about our programs. Meet our teachers and see our facilities.

2

Application Form

Fill out our admission form with your child's details and submit required documents.

3

Interaction Session

Your child will have a friendly interaction session with our teachers to assess readiness.

4

Confirmation

Receive admission confirmation and complete the enrollment process with fee payment.

Age Groups & Eligibility

Find the perfect program for your child's age and development stage

Playgroup

2 - 3 years

  • Basic motor skills development
  • Social interaction introduction
  • Sensory play activities
  • Music and movement

Nursery

3 - 4 years

  • Independence building
  • Pre-reading skills
  • Creative arts and crafts
  • Basic counting and shapes

LKG (Lower Kindergarten)

4 - 5 years

  • Reading and writing readiness
  • Mathematical concepts
  • Science exploration
  • Leadership skills

UKG (Upper Kindergarten)

5 - 6 years

  • School readiness preparation
  • Advanced literacy skills
  • Problem-solving abilities
  • Confidence building

Admission Requirements

Documents and information needed for enrollment

Required Documents

  • Birth certificate of the child
  • Immunization records
  • Recent passport-size photographs (2 copies)
  • Parent/Guardian ID proof
  • Address proof
  • Previous school records (if applicable)
  • Medical certificate (if required)
