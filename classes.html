Our Curriculum

Comprehensive learning approach covering all developmental areas

Cognitive Development

Building thinking skills, problem-solving abilities, and logical reasoning through age-appropriate activities and challenges.

Emotional Growth

Developing self-awareness, emotional regulation, empathy, and positive relationships with peers and adults.

Physical Development

Enhancing gross and fine motor skills, coordination, balance, and overall physical fitness through play and exercise.

Language & Communication

Building vocabulary, communication skills, listening abilities, and early literacy through stories, songs, and conversations.

Creative Expression

Encouraging imagination, artistic abilities, musical appreciation, and creative thinking through various art forms.

Social Awareness

Understanding community, culture, environment, and developing respect for diversity and inclusion.
